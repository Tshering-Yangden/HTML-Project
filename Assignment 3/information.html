INFOMATION OF SPICES

Phal. Charming Crystal Water

    This pure white hybrid has the exciting mutation of being peloric, as it possesses three petals and no lip! A fantastic compact type with impressively large flowers that will be 3" wide, up to 5" wide on older specimens! Easy to grow and flower while remaining compact in stature, this orchid will provide several months of beautiful and unique flowers. Please Note: Current batch are in 3" round clear production pots and are the perfect size to slip into a decorative container.

Phal. I-Lan Golden Tree

    This is a great yellow with red lip Phalaenopsis that has branching spikes creating a wonderful display of yellow and red 3-3.5" flowers. These are extremely long-lasting flowers and the spikes can often flower for 3-5 months. The presentation of the flowers is excellent as well with just the right spacing between the flowers.


Hal. I-Hsin Sunflower x P. manii Black Magic

    An interesting cross with the famous black manii crossed onto a modern day hybrid. These seed-grown hybrids produce yellow flowers with markings in red branching sprays.


Phal. Perfect Pixie

     A really nice little mini Phal. with bright magenta flowers. Easy to grow and flower. Can flower at any time of year. These will stay small and can flower twice per year. Perfect! Interestingly, many open purple and fade to a light pink while retaining the purple column as the flowers age.
